Crochet Yarn

Different yarn is needed for making different items. One yarn will be used for make socks such as bulky and other yarn such as sports weight will be used for making clothes and blankets.
Super bulky is used for items such as hats and scarves. There are 6 main types of yarn that can be used to make different projects as defined below.

Crochet Yarn Types

  1. Super Fine (sock or baby fingering yarn)
  2. Fine (sports yarn)
  3. Light (light worsted and DK yarn)
  4. Medium (worsted)
  5. Bulky (chunky)
  6. Super Bulky(roving)
